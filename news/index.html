  • 2025-05-01 VolksWagenstiftung: Kerstin has received a highly competitive Momentum grant from the Volkswagen Foundation (almost EUR 1 million!). With this grant, we will establish a state-of-the-art iPSC platform for the generation and use of human skin organoids over a period of four years. This platform will enable us to model human skin in the laboratory and thus investigate the mechanisms of skin regeneration and the causes of skin diseases.
